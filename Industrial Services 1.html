EXCHANGER OVERHAULING

We extract bundle safely by using a Bundle extractor or a Trolley mounted chain blocks when there is a provision. Different Bundle Pullers are used depending on the dimensions, elevation and weight of the bundle that has to be removed. We have Skilled and Experienced Technicians for these high precision jobs.

SUPER SUCKER VACUUM TRUCK

Our Super Sucker Vacuum Trucks offer a Safe and Reliable Solution for cleaning up Dry and Wet Industrial Material like Sludge, Slurries, Oil, Solids in the form of Granules and Powder in a Environment friendly way.

CHEMICAL CLEANING
